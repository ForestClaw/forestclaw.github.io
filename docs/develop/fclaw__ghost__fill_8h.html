void fclaw_face_neighbor_ghost ( struct fclaw_global *  glob,
int  minlevel,
int  maxlevel,
int  time_interp 
)

Complete exchange of all ghost patches at all levels.

All parallel ghost patches are also exchanged at all levels.

  • Every level exchanges ghost cells with other patches at that level
  • Every finer level exchanges with a coarser level
  • All levels will be updated in next update step, regardless of whether we are in the subcycled or non-subcycled case.

The reason for two separate ghost cell exchange routines is that the logic here is considerably simpler than for the partial update used in intermediate steps in the subcycled case.
